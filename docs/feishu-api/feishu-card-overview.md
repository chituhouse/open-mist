# Overview of Feishu Cards

This document introduces the basic concepts, features, and application scenarios of Feishu cards, helping you to quickly get started with Feishu cards.

## What are Feishu Cards

Feishu cards are a capability of applications, including the components needed to build card content and the capabilities required to send cards, and provide a [visual building tool](https://open.feishu.cn/cardkit?from=open_docs_overview).

Feishu cards can embed structured content into Feishu collaboration scenarios such as chat messages, group pinned messages, and link previews, enhancing the efficiency of information delivery. Users can perform business processes through simple interactions on the card, making business systems readily accessible. In short, Feishu cards transform the application usage experience from "people finding applications" to "applications finding people."

## Features

Feishu cards can **structure** your graphic and text content and **embed** it into business collaboration scenarios, while also enabling **lightweight interactions**, **drawing charts**, **multi-scenario reuse**, and other capabilities. Specifically, the functional features of Feishu cards can be summarized as follows.

### **Structured**

Feishu cards provide flexible layout capabilities and a rich array of graphic and text presentation components, helping you to organize card content more structurally and enhance information delivery efficiency.

### **Embedded**

Feishu cards can be embedded into chat messages, group pins, link previews, and other Feishu collaboration scenarios, making business systems readily accessible.

### **Light interaction**

Feishu cards support "click to submit" lightweight interaction capabilities in business. Users can click on the interactive components of the Feishu card in Feishu conversations, link previews, and other scenarios to complete business processes. For example, through Feishu chat notifications, complete work order approval and transfer, event registration and voting, form filling and submission, and other interactions.

### **Data visualization**

Feishu cards provide chart components, which can be used to draw various types of charts such as bar charts, line charts, and pie charts, enriching the presentation of business data. For details, refer to [Chart](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukzMukzMukzM/feishu-cards/card-json-v2-components/content-components/chart).

### **Multi-scenario reuse**

Cards built using the visual building tool are also known as card templates. Each card template has a unique card template ID, generated by the building tool. You can use this ID to send the same card in different conversations or group chats, achieving multi-scenario reuse. Additionally, the building tool supports the card variable feature, which allows you to assign actual business scenario values to these variables, enabling multi-scenario reuse of cards. For details, refer to [Configure Card Variables](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukzMukzMukzM/feishu-cards/feishu-card-cardkit/configure-card-variables).

## Application scenarios

Feishu cards can be embedded in chat messages, group pinned messages, link previews, and other Feishu collaboration scenarios to enhance information delivery efficiency. Common use cases and explanations are provided below.

### Message scenarios

When sending messages, cards can be sent to individuals or groups as a type of message. In message scenarios, Feishu cards are widely used, including static content push, dynamic content interaction, and AI Bot scenarios.

#### **Static Content Push**

In static content push scenarios, cards mainly contain static display information such as text, images, personnel, and charts, and may also include buttons to open specified links.

Specific scenario examples: Pushing industry news, sales performance rankings, employee birthday wishes, business monitoring alerts, etc.

In static content push scenarios, based on different business needs, you can refer to the following tutorials and the [reference cases](http://open.feishu.cn/cardkit?catalogId=10014&from=open_docs) in the build tool case library to create and send cards:

#### **Dynamic Content Interaction**

In dynamic content interaction scenarios, users can "submit with a click" in the card, and the card needs to receive the submitted data and respond accordingly.

Specific scenario examples: Ticket approval and workflow, event registration and voting, form filling and submission. In this scenario, it is recommended to refer to the [Develop a Card Interactive Bot](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/uMzNwEjLzcDMx4yM3ATM/develop-a-card-interactive-bot/introduction) tutorial to implement user data submission and card updates.

#### **AI Bot**

The AI Bot scenario refers to when users interact with an AI Bot through chat, and the AI Bot sends streaming update cards, adds, deletes, or modifies components in the card to interact with users in real-time. In this scenario, you can refer to the [Streaming Update OpenAPI Call Guide](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukzMukzMukzM/feishu-cards/streaming-updates-openapi-overview) to achieve similar effects.

### Message link preview scenarios

Feishu cards can be associated with the [Link Preview](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukzMukzMukzM/development-link-preview/link-preview-development-guide) capability of the Feishu Open Platform, enabling specific links in Feishu chat messages and group pins to preview content through Feishu cards. This allows users to intuitively understand the information contained in the link without navigating away. To implement content preview through cards, it is recommended to refer to the [Link Preview Development Guide](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukzMukzMukzM/development-link-preview/link-preview-development-guide) tutorial.

### Group pin card scenarios

Feishu cards support display in pinned messages of Feishu chats. You can select the **Group Pin Card Scenario** in the [Card Build Tool](https://open.feishu.cn/cardkit?from=open_docs_send_feishu_card) to preview the effect.

## Basic concepts

The following concepts may be involved when using Lark cards.
- **Card template**: A card created based on the [Card Builder](https://open.feishu.cn/cardkit?from=open_docs_overview) is referred to as a card template.
- **Card template ID** (`template_id`): In the Card Builder, each card template has a unique card ID, which can be obtained from the top menu bar of the Card Builder, as shown in the following image:

![](//sf3-cn.feishucdn.com/obj/open-platform-opendoc/e1bb29c4738274638ee0f8e6debaaf30_eObXe5uGsu.png?height=322&lazyload=true&maxWidth=500&width=1725)

- **Card entity**: A card created based on the [Create Card Entity](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukTMukTMukTM/cardkit-v1/card/create) API is referred to as a card entity.
- **Card entity ID** (`card_id`): When calling the [Card Resource Server API](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukTMukTMukTM/cardkit-v1/feishu-card-resource-overview#791c8e74) to send or update a card, the `card_id` is required to specify the card, representing the unique identifier of the card entity.
- **Card example**: Card examples in the reference case library of the Card Builder.
